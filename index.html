Wombat

A modern graphical interface for the Numbat programming language — scientific computing with first-class units, dimensions, and effortless conversions. Available on GNU/Linux.

Wombat brings the power of Numbat to a friendly desktop interface. Use the precision of a unit-aware programming language without needing a terminal.

Write expressions naturally, combine units safely, and convert results instantly. Ideal for scientists, engineers, students, and anyone who works with real-world quantities.
